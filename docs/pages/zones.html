Zones & Points of Interest

Organize the camp into geographical Zones and specific Points of Interest (POIs) to structure tours and track visitor interest.

Zones

Zones represent broad areas within the camp, such as "Market", "Education", or "Community".

Managing Zones

  • Create: Click "Add Zone", provide a name and description.
  • Edit: Update zone details or toggle active status.
  • Delete: Remove a zone (only if no POIs are attached).

Points of Interest (POIs)

POIs are specific locations within a Zone that tours visit, such as "Main Market Stall", "Primary School", or "Health Center".

Managing POIs

  1. Select a Zone to view its POIs.
  2. Click Add POI.
  3. Enter the name, description, and category.
  4. Save to add it to the zone.

Meeting Points

Designated locations where tours start. These are selected during the booking process.

  • Welcome Center: Main entrance meeting point.
  • Community Hall: Alternative meeting point.
Tip: Use Zones to analyze visitor traffic. The Dashboard shows which zones are most popular based on completed tours.
