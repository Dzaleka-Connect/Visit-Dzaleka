Reports & Analytics

Gain insights into camp visits, revenue, and operational performance.

Revenue Reports

Track financial performance over time.

  • Total Revenue: Aggregate earnings from all tours.
  • Monthly Breakdown: Revenue by month.
  • By Payment Method: Split between Cash, Airtel Money, and TNM Mpamba.

Visit Analytics

Understand visitor behavior and preferences.

  • Popular Zones: Which areas receive the most visits.
  • Peak Times: Busiest days and times for tours.
  • Group Sizes: Distribution of individual vs. group bookings.

Guide Performance

Monitor guide activity and quality.

  • Tour Count: Number of tours completed per guide.
  • Ratings: Average visitor feedback score.
  • Completion Rate: Percentage of assigned tours successfully completed.
